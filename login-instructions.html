There are two ways to log in to the Cornerstone Learning Management System.

For employees

Use your standard Children’s Mercy computer username and password. Do not use your old CHEX username that started with “cmh”. If the username is locked-in and incorrect, click “Use another account.”

Cornerstone - Single Sign On



For contracted staff, volunteers, etc.

Use "x017." + your id number as your username (for example, x017.1234567), or use your @cmh.edu email address. The default password you will change when first logging on is Success45. Click the link for Forgot Password? if your password is not recognized.
